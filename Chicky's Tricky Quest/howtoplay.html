Rules of the Game "Chicky's Tricky Quest"

Embark on a cluck-tastic adventure with "Chicky's Tricky Quest," a charming puzzle-platformer set on a sunny farm. Help Chicky climb through challenging levels using her wobbly legs, collect eggs, and unlock farmyard secrets. With customizable settings, a coop shop, and bonus rewards - can you master the art of egg-cellent navigation?

How to Play

The rules are simple but egg-stremely fun:

  • Guide Chicky through farm-themed levels using precise claw movements
  • Collect all golden eggs in each level to unlock the exit gate
  • Use special abilities from the Coop Shop to overcome tricky obstacles
  • Avoid hazards like hay bales, mud pits, and pesky farmhands
  • Complete bonus challenges for extra rewards

Features

  • Explore 30+ handcrafted levels ranging from chicken runs to windmill rooftops
  • Customize your gameplay with sound settings and visual effects
  • Visit the Coop Shop to purchase power-ups and cosmetic upgrades
  • Unlock bonus mini-games and secret farm areas
  • Compete for high scores in speed-run mode
  • Enjoy whimsical farmyard visuals and cluck-tastic sound effects

"Chicky's Tricky Quest" combines precision platforming with farm-fresh puzzles. As you progress through increasingly complex levels, you'll need to master Chicky's unique movement style while strategically using your egg collection. The game combines classic platforming challenges with modern quality-of-life features like customizable controls and a reward system that lets you upgrade your feathery friend. Can you help Chicky become the ultimate farm explorer?
